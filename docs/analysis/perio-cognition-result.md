# Periodontitis ↔ cognition — the neuro-axis empirical anchor (NHANES 2011-2012)

> Run 2026-07-10. `run_perio_cognition.py --exposure perio_cal`. NHANES 2011-2012, 9756 participants
> merged, n=919 with complete perio + cognition + confounders (the cognition module is age 60+).
> Confounder-adjusted standardized OLS (age, education, smoking, HbA1c) + seeded bootstrap 90% CI.
> Report: `results/perio_cognition_report.json`. **Non-diagnostic, population-level.**

## Result: a real, confounder-adjusted association in the direction the model predicts

Exposure = mean periodontal attachment loss (CAL). Coefficients are standardized (SD units); a
**negative** value = worse cognition with more periodontal severity.

| cognitive outcome | crude | **adjusted** | CI90 (adj) | verdict |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Digit Symbol (processing speed) | −0.324 | **−0.181** | [−0.226, −0.137] | **SIGNIFICANT** |
| Animal Fluency (executive) | −0.172 | **−0.098** | [−0.157, −0.045] | **SIGNIFICANT** |
| CERAD immediate recall | −0.153 | **−0.057** | [−0.108, −0.007] | **SIGNIFICANT** |
| CERAD delayed recall | −0.126 | **−0.048** | [−0.100, +0.002] | ns (CI touches 0) |

**3 of 4 cognitive measures show a significant, confounder-adjusted negative association** between
periodontal severity and cognition — higher CAL → worse cognition, even after adjusting for the
dominant confounders (age, education, smoking, HbA1c). The direction matches what `mech_neuro`
predicts (oral inflammation → neuroinflammation → cognitive burden). Processing speed (Digit Symbol)
shows the strongest adjusted effect.

## Honest reading — what it is and is NOT

- **It is real and adjusted.** The association survives adjustment for age/education/smoking/HbA1c on
  3/4 measures, with bootstrap CIs excluding 0. This is the neuro axis's **empirical anchor** — the
  association the mechanistic model predicts is *present* in real, public, de-identified data.
- **Adjustment roughly halves the effect** (crude → adjusted), i.e. **much of the crude association is
  confounding** (age above all). This is the honest picture: a modest true-adjusted signal, not the
  large crude one. Effect sizes (−0.05 to −0.18 SD) are small — consistent with the epidemiology
  (E4.6) and with this being one contributor among many.
- **It is an ASSOCIATION, not causation, and not a measured mechanism.** Cross-sectional; reverse
  causation is live (early cognitive decline → worse oral self-care → periodontitis). The inflammation
  mediator that `mech_neuro` models is **not in-cycle** with cognition (CRP was discontinued after
  2010), so this measures *whether the predicted association exists*, not *why*. The "why" remains the
  modeled hypothesis; this is the "whether," and it holds.
- **cerad_delayed is not significant** (CI [−0.100, +0.002], just touches 0) — reported as-is, not
  rounded into the win.
- Residual confounding (SES beyond education, general vascular/metabolic health) is unadjusted.
